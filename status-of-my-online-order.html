STATUS OF MY ONLINE ORDER

You can track your order in your account, via the Purchases section. All of these conditions are the stages your order will go through: Purchased : You have successfully completed the correct purchase of your order. Orders in progress : we have received information about your purchase. In preparation : your order is currently being prepared in the warehouse. Shipped : the order has departed from our facility. Picked up by courier : your order is currently in transit, and you will receive it shortly. Sent to client : You have received your order. We remind you that you will receive updates on the status of your order via email.
