WHICH IS MY SIZE?

At Zara we offer a wide range of sizes which can vary depending on the style, cut and fabric of each garment. So you can choose the size that best suits you, we offer you two of our tools, namely the size guide and the Find your size option

SIZE GUIDE

On the product about page you can find our size guide . Choose the size you are interested in and we will show you various body sizes that can fit you.

FIND OUT YOUR SIZE

For guidance in choosing your size, we recommend using the Find your size option , available in the description of each item. Through this tool, we will calculate the most suitable size for you based on the data provided to us (your body size and the way you like to wear clothes.
