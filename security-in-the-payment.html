SECURITY IN THE PAYMENT PROCESS

The security of information that customers delegate to Zara.com is one of our highest priorities, therefore we invest significant resources and use the latest technology available so that your payment data is processed in a secure environment.

Our e-commerce platform is certified as good practice in information security management, as well as compliance with the Data Security for Payment Card Industry standard which verifies that we follow the highest industry recommendations and standards to provide you with a shopping experience in a private, honest and secure environment.
