COMPOSITION & VARIOUS TREATMENTS

At Zara, you can find various types of clothing from natural, artificial, and synthetic materials. Both on Zara.com and on the clothing, you will get detailed information about the contents of each item.

Our Animal Welfare Policy includes various ethical standards for the use of products of animal origin in accordance with the “Five Principles” of animal welfare for the ethical and thorough treatment of animals. You can get more information here.

We work with various monitoring programs to ensure compliance with all our social, environmental standards as well as for the safety and health of each of our garments.

To evaluate compliance, we have developed an audit program and continuous improvement plans.

You can visit our Join Life section for more information.

VARIOUS RECOMMENDATIONS FOR CARE

In each product, both on the web and on the labels of our garments, you can find information for the optimal type of care for each of your garments.

Additionally, we have created a guide that will help you care for your clothes and extend their life. Visit take care of your clothes for more information.
