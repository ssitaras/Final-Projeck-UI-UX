MY FAVORITES

With the options Favorite and Save for another occasion , you'll be able to bookmark and easily access the items you like most. Here's how to use it.

With this option, you will be able to save every item you are interested in . How does it work?

  • Every time you click on the Favorites symbol that you will find on the product page, the item will be added to the list .
  • You will be able to access your list entry from your shopping cart and share it with anyone you want via the link we will provide when you select this option.
  • You will be able to delete any number of items whenever you want, simply by clicking on the symbol in Various Favorites .
  • The maximum capacity of the Various Favorites option is 100 items.
  • Marking an item as a favorite does not imply your reservation.

SAVE FOR ANOTHER OCCASION

If you do not wish to complete the purchase of all the items in your basket and prefer to leave them for another time, then we recommend that you use the Save for another occasion option . This makes it easy to add each of these items to a favorites list so you can retrieve them whenever you want.
