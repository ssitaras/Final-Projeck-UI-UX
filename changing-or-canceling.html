CHANGING OR CANCELING AN ONLINE ORDER

If you have placed an order and want to modify it, we answer your possible questions here.

CAN YOU CANCEL YOUR ORDER?

It is possible to cancel the purchase as long as the order status allows. If available, you will see this option in your Zara account, in the order details

CAN YOU CHANGE YOUR ORDER DELIVERY ADDRESS?

If you have selected home delivery, you can change your order delivery address as long as your order status allows. You can do this by accessing the order details from your account.

CAN ANY ITEMS IN YOUR ORDER BE MODIFIED?

Once a purchase has been completed, the item cannot be deleted or modified. If you wish, once you receive your order, you will be able to arrange a return.
